Merced River

TheMerced River(/mrsd/), in the central part of theU.S. stateofCalifornia, is a145-mile (233km)-longtributaryof theSan Joaquin Riverflowing from theSierra Nevadainto theSan Joaquin Valley. It is most well known for its swift and steep course through the southern part ofYosemite National Park, where it is the primary watercourse flowing throughYosemite Valley. The river's character changes dramatically once it reaches the plains of the agricultural San Joaquin Valley, where it becomes a slow-moving meandering stream.
